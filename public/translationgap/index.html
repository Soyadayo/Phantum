TranslationGap

English news articles are often based on sources written in other languages. When they get translated, details get dropped, emphasis shifts, and sometimes the meaning changes entirely. This tool checks what got lost.
How it works
1 You paste the link to an English news article below.
2 We find the original sources in Turkish, Arabic, Japanese, Persian, Hindi, or Portuguese.
3 We compare them and show you what the English version left out, got wrong, or framed differently.
