3. Data We Collect

Usage data. When you use Your Situation, a random anonymous user ID is generated and stored in your browser's local storage. This ID is used solely to track your reading credits. It is not linked to your name or identity.

IP address. Your IP address is temporarily used for rate limiting (to enforce the daily free reading limit). It is stored with a key that expires at midnight UTC each day. We do not use IP addresses for tracking or profiling.

Input text. The text you submit for a reading is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not store your input text on our servers. Google's data handling is governed by their Gemini API Terms of Service.

Payment data. If you purchase readings, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details. Stripe may collect your email address, payment method, and billing information. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Contact form. If you send us a message through the contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent to us via email. We use this data only to respond to your inquiry.

Haus Tacorari does not use cookies. Your Situation uses browser local storage to save your anonymous user ID and paid credit count. This data stays in your browser and is not transmitted to third parties.
